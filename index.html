About

Rate My Restrooms is an online platform for UC San Diego Students to rank and rate bathrooms across the different locations on campus. Our mission is to provide a safe forum to share restroom experiences and help students make critical restroom choices the next time they plan to go.

Guidelines

Restroom ratings can be posted by anyone who has used the designated facility.
